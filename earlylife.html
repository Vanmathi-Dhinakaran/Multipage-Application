Early Life

Dr. Kalam's journey began in Rameswaram, a small coastal town. Raised in a modest family, he imbibed values of hard work, perseverance, and a thirst for knowledge from his parents. Despite financial constraints, he pursued his education diligently. Dr. Kalam's academic journey led him to the Madras Institute of Technology, where he obtained a degree in Aeronautical Engineering. This foundation would serve as a springboard for his future accomplishments.
